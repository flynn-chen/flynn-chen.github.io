Smart & Sustainable Eating: Your Guide to Healthy Dieting

The core elements that make up a healthy dietary pattern include:

  • Vegetables of all types—at least 2 servings per day
    • Dark green/red/orange; beans, peas, and lentils; starchy; and other vegetables
  • Fruits—at least 2 servings a day
    • Especially whole fruit as they have great fiber!
  • Grains—at least 2 servings a day
    • At least half of which are whole grain
  • Dairy—at least 2 servings a day
    • Including fat-free or low-fat milk, yogurt, and cheese, and/or lactose-free versions and fortified soy beverages and yogurt as alternatives
  • Protein foods—Aim for 8 oz a day
    • Including lean meats, poultry, and eggs; seafood; beans, peas, and lentils; and nuts, seeds, and soy products
  • Oils
    • Including vegetable oils and oils in food, such as seafood, avocado and nuts

Potential eating patterns to consider: Mediterranean, DASH, lacto-ovo-vegetarian; more information at https://shorturl.at/pstD7 by scrolling down or searching on top right of the page.
